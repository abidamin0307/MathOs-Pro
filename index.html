About MathOS Pro

MathOS Pro is a comprehensive collection of 85+ fully tested calculators designed for students, engineers, professionals, and educators. Each calculator includes a detailed 50-100 word educational guide explaining the formula, its derivation, real-world applications, and underlying principles.

We believe mathematics should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to eliminate "black box" calculations by providing the mathematical theory alongside every result.
