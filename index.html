About MathOS Pro

MathOS Pro is a premium computational ecosystem engineered for professionals and students who demand more than just a number. Every tool is backed by an extensive educational guide designed to explain the structural significance of the math being performed. Founded in 2026, we aim to eliminate "black box" calculations by providing the theory alongside the result.

Privacy & Security

Our "Local-First" architecture means your data never leaves your machine. MathOS Pro performs all calculations client-side within your browser's V8 engine. We do not use databases, we do not track your input values, and we do not sell user behavior. Your intellectual work remains entirely yours.
